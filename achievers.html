Here is the list of Achievers from Gurukul Martial Arts and Yoga Academy!

At Gurukul Martial Arts and Yoga Academy, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive training in martial arts and yoga. With a focus on discipline, technique, and holistic well-being, our academy has produced over 200 students who have excelled in International, National, and State events across various disciplines including Karate, Kubudo, Wushu, Kickboxing, and Pankration.

Notable Achievements:

  • Meithli Rana: World Ranking player by WKF, International medalist in Karate and Kickboxing.
  • Ananya Rawat: International medalist in Indo Continental 2023 Agra in Karate.
  • Akshita Gurung: 2 gold medals in Karate in Indo Continental in Agra, 1 gold and 1 silver in South Asia Karate Championship Delhi 2024.
  • Somya Jha: Wushu State gold medalist, National player in Wushu, medallist in Indo continental in Agra.
  • Aghessiz: International medalist in Karate, State gold medalist in Kickboxing.
  • Kalpana: National medalist in All India championship.
  • Rishabh: National Kickboxing player, Medallist in International Karate championship.
